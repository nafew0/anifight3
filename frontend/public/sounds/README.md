# Sound Files for AniFight

This directory contains all audio files used in the game.

## Required Files

Place the following MP3 files in this directory:

### Tier Rating Sounds
- **tier-s.mp3** - Enthusiastic cheer for S-tier characters (90+ percentile)
- **tier-a.mp3** - Excited cheer for A-tier characters (70-90 percentile)
- **tier-b.mp3** - Clapping/applause for B-tier characters (40-70 percentile)
- **tier-c.mp3** - Neutral crowd sound for C-tier characters (10-40 percentile)
- **tier-d.mp3** - Sad trombone/disappointed sound for D-tier characters (<10 percentile)

### Result Sounds (Phase 7)
- **victory.mp3** - Win fanfare when player wins
- **defeat.mp3** - Defeat sound when player loses

## Sound Requirements

- **Format**: MP3 (best browser compatibility)
- **Duration**: 1-3 seconds maximum
- **File Size**: <100KB each (recommended)
- **Quality**: 128kbps or higher
- **License**: Royalty-free or properly licensed

## Sound Recommendations

### Tier S (S-tier)
- High-energy crowd cheer
- Exciting, enthusiastic tone
- Examples: stadium roar, epic fanfare

### Tier A (A-tier)
- Excited applause
- Positive, upbeat tone
- Examples: crowd cheering, happy fanfare

### Tier B (B-tier)
- Moderate applause
- Neutral-positive tone
- Examples: clapping, polite applause

### Tier C (C-tier)
- Neutral crowd reaction
- Indifferent tone
- Examples: scattered applause, "meh" sound

### Tier D (D-tier)
- Disappointed reaction
- Humorous, light-hearted negative tone
- Examples: sad trombone, "aww" from crowd

## Free Sound Resources

- **Freesound.org**: Community-uploaded sound effects (CC licensed)
- **Zapsplat.com**: Free sound effects library
- **Mixkit.co**: Free sound effects and music
- **BBC Sound Effects**: Archive of BBC sound effects

## Testing

The game will work without these files, but:
- No audio will play when characters are drawn
- Console warnings will appear when sounds fail to load
- All other functionality remains intact

Once you add the files, refresh the game to load them.
